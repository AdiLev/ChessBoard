## Quick Upload Guide

1. **App Icon:**
   - Play Console > Store listing > App icon
   - Upload: `icon/app-icon-512x512.png`

2. **Feature Graphic:**
   - Play Console > Store listing > Feature graphic
   - Upload: `feature-graphic/feature-graphic-1024x500.png`

3. **Screenshots:**
   - Play Console > Store listing > Screenshots
   - Upload all files from `screenshots/` folder
   - Order them logically (main screen first)
