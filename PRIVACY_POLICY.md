# Privacy Policy for ChessBoard

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

ChessBoard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ChessBoard handles information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**ChessBoard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection. All game data, including saved games, move history, and preferences, are stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device:
- Saved game files (.chb format) are stored in the app's private storage directory
- Move history and game state are stored in device memory during gameplay
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties

## Permissions

ChessBoard requires the following permissions:
- **Storage Access**: To save and load game files (.chb format) on your device
  - This permission is used only for local file storage
  - No data is shared with external services

## Third-Party Services

ChessBoard does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not integrate with social media platforms or external APIs.

## Children's Privacy

ChessBoard is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with children's privacy regulations.

## Data Security

Since ChessBoard does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breaches related to personal information. All game data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security measures.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app updates and this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since ChessBoard does not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All game data stored locally can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.
