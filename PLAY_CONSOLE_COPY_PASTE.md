## Full Description
```
ChessBoard - Your Complete Chess Experience

ChessBoard is a beautifully designed, full-featured chess game for Android that brings the classic game of chess to your mobile device. Whether you're a beginner learning the game or an experienced player looking for a reliable chess companion, ChessBoard offers everything you need for an enjoyable chess experience.

Key Features:

🎯 Complete Chess Rules
- All standard chess pieces with authentic movement rules
- Full pawn promotion support
- Kingside and Queenside castling (O-O, O-O-O)
- Proper move validation and turn management
- Check detection and game state tracking

📚 Move History & Navigation
- Complete move history tracking
- Interactive seekbar to navigate through moves
- Step forward and backward through game history
- Jump to first or last move instantly
- View move notation and game progression

💾 Save & Load Games
- Save your games as CHB files
- Load previously saved games
- Multiple game files support
- Never lose your progress

🎮 User-Friendly Interface
- Clean, modern design
- Intuitive touch controls
- Visual move highlights
- Captured pieces display
- Fullscreen mode for focused gameplay

✨ Additional Features
- Auto-play mode for reviewing games
- Pause/resume functionality
- Responsive board that adapts to screen size
- Beautiful piece rendering with detailed graphics
- Smooth animations and interactions

Perfect For:
- Chess enthusiasts of all skill levels
- Players learning chess rules and strategies
- Anyone who wants to practice chess on the go
- Teachers and students studying chess
- Casual players looking for a quality chess app

Why Choose ChessBoard?

ChessBoard combines classic chess gameplay with modern mobile features. The app respects all official chess rules while providing an intuitive interface that makes playing chess enjoyable and accessible. With save/load functionality, you can pause your game anytime and resume later, making it perfect for busy schedules.

The move history feature allows you to review your games, learn from mistakes, and improve your chess skills. Whether you're analyzing a game or simply replaying an interesting match, ChessBoard gives you full control over your chess experience.

Technical Details:
- Minimum Android version: 7.0 (API 24)
- Target Android version: 14 (API 34)
- Optimized for all screen sizes
- Lightweight and efficient
- No internet connection required
- No ads or in-app purchases

Privacy & Data:
ChessBoard respects your privacy. The app does not collect personal information, does not require internet access, and all game data is stored locally on your device. Your games remain private and secure.

Start playing chess today with ChessBoard - where classic gameplay meets modern convenience!
```
